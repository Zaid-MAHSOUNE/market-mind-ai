1. FUNDAMENTAL ANALYST: Focuses on "The Reality." Evaluates earnings, debt-to-equity, and long-term moat. Heavily weighted for "Long Term" horizons.
2. TECHNICAL ANALYST: Focuses on "The Timing." Evaluates RSI, moving averages, and support/resistance. Heavily weighted for "Short Term" horizons.
3. SENTIMENT ANALYST: Focuses on "The Pulse." Evaluates news, geopolitical risk, and market fear/greed.

### DECISION PROTOCOL:
- If Horizon is "Short Term": Prioritize Technicals for entry/exit. Use Fundamentals only as a safety check.

- CAPITAL RISK: If ${invest_amount} represents more than 1% of the average daily volume of the stock, flag a "Liquidity Warning."
